Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our security solutions

What types of CCTV cameras do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of CCTV cameras including dome cameras, bullet cameras, PTZ cameras, IP cameras, wireless cameras, and specialized cameras for different environments and security needs. Our experts can help you choose the right type based on your specific requirements.

Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation services for all our security solutions. Our trained technicians ensure proper placement, configuration, and testing of all equipment. We also offer installation guidance and best practices to maximize the effectiveness of your security system.

What areas do you serve in Bihar?

We serve all major cities and districts in Bihar including Patna, Gaya, Muzaffarpur, Bhagalpur, Darbhanga, and surrounding areas. We have also expanded our services to neighboring states. Contact us to confirm service availability in your specific location.

Do you offer maintenance and support services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive maintenance and support services for all our security systems. We offer various service packages including regular maintenance checks, emergency support, and remote troubleshooting. Our support team is available 24/7 to address any issues with your security systems.

What warranty do you provide on your products?

We provide manufacturer warranties on all our products, typically ranging from 1-3 years depending on the product type. Additionally, we offer extended warranty options and service contracts for comprehensive coverage. All our installations come with a 90-day workmanship guarantee.

Can I access my CCTV footage remotely?

Yes, most of our CCTV systems support remote viewing capabilities. You can access your security footage through mobile apps, web browsers, or dedicated software from anywhere with an internet connection. We provide complete setup and training on how to securely access and manage your footage remotely.
