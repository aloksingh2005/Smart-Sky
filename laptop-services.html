Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our laptop services

How long does a typical laptop repair take?

Most common repairs like screen replacements, keyboard fixes, and software issues can be resolved within 24-48 hours. Complex repairs like motherboard issues may take 3-5 days. We always provide an estimated timeline when you bring in your laptop.

Do you provide warranty on your repairs?

Yes, we provide a 30-day warranty on all our repair services. For parts replacement, we offer the manufacturer's warranty or our own 3-month warranty, whichever is longer.

What brands of laptops do you repair?

We repair all major laptop brands including Dell, HP, Lenovo, Acer, Asus, Apple MacBooks, Microsoft Surface, Samsung, Toshiba, and many more.

Do you buy old laptops?

Yes, we purchase old laptops in working or non-working condition. The price depends on the laptop's age, specifications, and overall condition. Bring your laptop to our store for a free evaluation and quote.

What warranty do you offer on second-hand laptops?

All our second-hand laptops come with a 3-month warranty covering hardware issues. We thoroughly test each laptop before selling to ensure it meets our quality standards.
