Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our printer services

How long does a typical printer repair take?

Most common printer issues can be resolved within a few hours. More complex repairs might take 1-2 days. We always provide an estimated timeline when you bring in your printer.

Do you provide warranty on your repairs?

Yes, we provide a 30-day warranty on all our repair services. For parts replacement, we offer the manufacturer's warranty or our own 3-month warranty, whichever is longer.

What brands of printers do you repair?

We repair all major printer brands including HP, Canon, Epson, Brother, Samsung, Xerox, Lexmark, Ricoh, and many more.

Do you offer on-site printer repairs for businesses?

Yes, we offer on-site repair services for businesses with multiple printers or large commercial printers that cannot be easily transported. Contact us to schedule an on-site visit.

Do you sell original or compatible ink cartridges?

We sell both original manufacturer cartridges and high-quality compatible cartridges. Compatible cartridges are more affordable while still providing good print quality. We can help you choose the best option based on your needs and budget.
